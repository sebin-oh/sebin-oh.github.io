2024 NHERI Computational Symposium Presentation

On February 1-2, 2024, Sebin Oh presented his research about Fragility Field for the Performance-Based Earthquake Engineering on a Regional Scale. The presentation focused mainly on introduction of the Ising model from statistical mechanics to the field of regional seismic analysis, providing a promising remedy to the issues in developing a joint probability distribution for the damage states of structures while applying the Performance-Nased Earthquake Engineering (PBEE) framework to a regional scale.
